What was our objective?

To analyse the listed directors of UK companies to find out what proportion were male / female

What was our methodology?

The total size of the data-set was all companies registered with Companies House in the UK

We then narrowed this data-set to companies who were obliged to publish gender pay gap information.

This is a requirement for all companies with 250 or more employees

Some companies with fewer than 250 employess have voluntarily published this data, they have also been included

Some questions to ask/consider

What is the size of the given data?

10,513 companies

Are we dealing with explicit numbers eg $xk per year or representative numbers?

For company directors, we have access to specific numbers eg 10 directors, 5 males and 5 females

For pay data (salaries and bonuses), we have representative data.

For example, pay is divided into 4 quartiles, (lower, lower-middle, upper-middle and upper).
